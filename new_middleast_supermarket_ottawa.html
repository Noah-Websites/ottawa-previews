About Us

Welcome to New Middleast Supermarket, your trusted destination for authentic Middle Eastern groceries in Alta Vista, Ottawa. For over 10 years, we have been serving the community with passion and dedication, providing the finest quality products from the Middle East and Mediterranean regions.

Our store specializes in halal meats, fresh produce, premium spices, and specialty ingredients that bring the flavors of the Middle East to your kitchen. We work directly with trusted suppliers to ensure every product meets our high standards of quality and authenticity.

With nearly 450 positive Google reviews, we have built a strong reputation as the go-to grocery store for families seeking genuine Middle Eastern products. Our knowledgeable staff is always ready to help you find exactly what you need.
