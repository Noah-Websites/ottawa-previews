From Homs to Ottawa, With Love

Jasmine Bakery was born from a simple dream: to share the authentic flavours of Syria with our new home in Ottawa. Since opening our doors in 2022, we've brought the same recipes our family has perfected for generations — baked fresh every single day.

Our kitchen is the heart of everything we do. From our signature manakeesh and freshly baked pita to our slow-roasted shawarma and rich fattoush, every dish tells a story of tradition, care, and love.

We believe food has the power to connect people, bridge cultures, and turn strangers into family. Whether it's your first visit or you're already part of the Jasmine family, there's always a warm meal and a warmer welcome waiting for you.
