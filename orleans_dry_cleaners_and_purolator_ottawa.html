About Us

Your Neighbourhood Service Centre

Orleans Dry Cleaners And Purolator has been proudly serving the Orleans community on Innes Road for over a decade. We offer the unique convenience of professional dry cleaning and Purolator courier services under one roof, saving our customers valuable time on their busy days.

With 71 Google reviews from satisfied customers, we have built a strong reputation for reliable service and quality garment care. Our experienced team understands the fabrics and techniques needed to keep your clothes looking sharp and lasting longer.

Whether you are dropping off a suit for professional cleaning, shipping an important package through Purolator, or picking up a delivery, we make it easy and efficient. Our convenient location inside the Ultramar plaza on Innes Road means you can take care of multiple errands in one stop.

What We Offer

Our Services

Dry Cleaning

Expert dry cleaning for suits, dresses, coats, and delicate garments. We treat every piece with care to preserve its quality and appearance.

Shirt Laundering

Professional shirt laundering with pressing and folding. Crisp, clean results for your everyday dress shirts and business wear.

Purolator Shipping

Send and receive packages with full Purolator courier services. Domestic and international shipping with tracking and insurance options.

Alterations

Basic tailoring and alterations available on-site. Hemming, taking in, and minor adjustments to keep your wardrobe fitting perfectly.

Pressing Service

Professional pressing for wrinkle-free garments ready to wear. Ideal for suits, dress pants, and formal attire that needs a crisp finish.

Package Pickup

Convenient Purolator package pickup and drop-off location. Skip the line at the post office and handle your shipping needs with ease.
