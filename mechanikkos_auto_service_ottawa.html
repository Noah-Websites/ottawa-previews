Family-Owned Expertise You Can Trust

Mechanikko's Auto Service Centre is a proud family-owned business in Gloucester, Ottawa, operated by Gabriel and his son. With a deep passion for automotive excellence and years of hands-on experience, they've built a reputation for honest, reliable, and professional service that keeps customers coming back.

What sets Mechanikko's apart is their commitment to quality workmanship and customer satisfaction. Every vehicle that comes through the shop receives the same level of care and attention to detail, whether it's routine maintenance or complex repairs. They take pride in their work and stand behind every service they provide.

The father-and-son team understands that vehicle repairs can be stressful and expensive. That's why they focus on transparent communication, fair pricing, and only recommending work that's truly necessary. Their perfect 5.0-star rating on Google with over 105 reviews reflects their dedication to excellence and customer service.
