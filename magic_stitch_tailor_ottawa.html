Kanata's Most Trusted Name in Tailoring

Located in the heart of Terry Fox Shopping Centre in Hazeldean, Magic Stitch And Tailor has been serving the Kanata community with exceptional tailoring and alterations for years. With over 300 five-star reviews, our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction is unmatched.

Under the expert guidance of Helen and her skilled team, we specialize in wedding dress alterations, custom tailoring, and precision hemming. Every garment is treated with the utmost care and attention to detail, ensuring your clothing fits perfectly and looks absolutely stunning.

Whether you need a simple hem or a complete custom suit, our passion for the craft and dedication to excellence makes Magic Stitch And Tailor your go-to destination for all your tailoring needs in Ottawa.

Our Services

Wedding Dress Alterations

Expert alterations for your special day. We handle bustles, trains, hemming, and intricate beadwork with precision and care.

Suit Tailoring

Professional tailoring for suits, blazers, and dress shirts. Achieve the perfect fit with our expert craftsmanship.

Custom Dressmaking

Bring your vision to life. We create custom dresses tailored to your measurements and style preferences.

Zipper & Repair Work

Quick and reliable repairs including zipper replacements, seam repairs, and garment restoration.

Hemming & Tapering

Perfect hemming for pants, skirts, and dresses. Professional tapering to achieve the ideal silhouette.

Dry Cleaning

Complete dry cleaning services to keep your garments fresh and looking their best year-round.
