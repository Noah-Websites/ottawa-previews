Our Story

A Perfect Five Stars for a Reason

La Zone 23 is a neighbourhood restaurant tucked away on Mann Avenue in the heart of Sandy Hill. With a perfect 5.0-star rating on Google, we have earned the loyalty of our community through one simple principle: every dish that leaves our kitchen must be something we would proudly serve to our own family.

Our menu features fresh, flavourful dishes prepared with care and attention to detail. From hearty mains to lighter bites, every item is cooked to order using quality ingredients. We take pride in offering generous portions at fair prices because we believe great food should be accessible to everyone in our community.

Whether you are a University of Ottawa student looking for a satisfying lunch, a Sandy Hill local grabbing dinner after work, or someone exploring Ottawa's hidden gems, La Zone 23 welcomes you with warm hospitality and food that keeps you coming back. Come visit us on Mann Avenue and taste the difference for yourself.

What We Serve

Our Menu Highlights

Grilled Plates

Juicy grilled meats and kebabs served with rice, fresh salads, and house-made sauces. Cooked over open flame for maximum flavour.

Fresh Salads

Crisp, vibrant salads made with seasonal vegetables and our signature dressings. A perfect light meal or a complement to any main dish.

Wraps and Sandwiches

Generous wraps packed with flavour and freshness. Ideal for a quick lunch or a satisfying meal on the go.

Soups and Stews

Comforting, slow-cooked soups and stews made from scratch daily. Perfect for warming up during Ottawa's cold winter months.

Beverages

A selection of fresh juices, hot drinks, and specialty beverages to complement your meal or enjoy on their own.

Takeout and Delivery

Enjoy La Zone 23 from the comfort of your home. Order takeout directly or through your favourite delivery app.
