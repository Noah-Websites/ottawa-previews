Premium Cuts and Classic Grooming in the Heart of Beechwood Village

Your Neighbourhood Barber on Beechwood

Welcome to Almos Barbershop, where tradition meets modern grooming excellence. Located in the heart of Vanier's Beechwood Village, we've been serving the Ottawa community with pride and professionalism. Ali, our owner and master barber, brings years of expertise and a passion for the craft to every cut and shave.

At Almos Barbershop, we believe in the timeless art of barbering. Whether you're looking for a sharp fade, a classic trim, or a full grooming experience with our signature hot towel shave, our skilled team is committed to delivering exceptional results and outstanding customer service. We treat every client like family.

Our barbershop is more than just a place to get a haircut. It's a gathering spot for the neighbourhood where quality craftsmanship is celebrated and relationships are built. We take pride in our 4.8-star rating and the trust our community has placed in us. Visit us today and experience the Almos Barbershop difference.

Our Services

Men's Haircuts

Classic and contemporary styles tailored to your preferences and face shape. Our expert barbers ensure precision cuts every time.

Beard Trim & Shaping

Define and maintain your beard with professional shaping, edging, and conditioning treatments that enhance your appearance.

Hot Towel Shave

Experience our signature hot towel shave service. A relaxing, traditional grooming experience with premium products and expert technique.

Kids' Cuts

We make haircuts fun for the little ones. Family-friendly environment with experienced barbers who know how to work with children.

Hair Styling

From pompadours to textured crops, our stylists create looks that suit your personality and lifestyle with premium products.

Fade & Taper

Expert fade and taper services using clippers and blades. Clean lines and smooth transitions that showcase professional craftsmanship.
