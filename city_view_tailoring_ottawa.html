About Us

A Tradition of Craftsmanship Since the 1980s

City View Tailoring has been serving the Nepean community for over 40 years, establishing itself as a cornerstone of expert craftsmanship and personalized service. Owner George Mahoty has dedicated his career to providing exceptional tailoring and alterations that exceed every client's expectations. With decades of experience in the industry, we've built a reputation for precision, attention to detail, and a commitment to preserving the art of traditional tailoring.

Whether you need custom suits tailored to your exact measurements, wedding attire alterations for your special day, or simple hemming and repairs, our skilled team treats every garment with the care and expertise it deserves. We work with a wide variety of fabrics including delicate silks, heavy wools, leather, and fur, ensuring that no project is too complex or unique for our accomplished craftspeople.

Our clients return to us year after year because we understand that great tailoring is about more than just adjusting seams. It's about enhancing your appearance, boosting your confidence, and ensuring your clothes fit perfectly to your body and lifestyle. In a world of mass production, City View Tailoring remains dedicated to the personal touch and quality that only a true neighborhood tailor can provide.

Our Services

Expert tailoring and alterations for every need

Custom Suits

Bespoke suit tailoring designed specifically to your measurements and style preferences. We create suits that fit perfectly and make you feel confident.

Dress Alterations

Professional alterations for dresses of all styles. From hemming to reshaping, we ensure your dresses fit flawlessly for any occasion.

Zipper Replacement

Expert zipper repair and replacement services. We fix or replace broken zippers on all types of clothing and accessories to restore functionality.

Hemming & Tapering

Precision hemming and tapering services for pants, skirts, and more. We ensure perfect length and fit that complements your body shape.

Leather & Fur Work

Specialized services for delicate leather jackets, coats, and fur garments. Our expertise ensures these premium items are handled with utmost care.

Wedding Attire

Complete alterations for wedding day attire. Whether wedding dress, suit, or bridesmaid gowns, we ensure everything is perfect for your special day.
