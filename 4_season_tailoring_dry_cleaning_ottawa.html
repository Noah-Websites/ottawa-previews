About Us

Two Decades of Excellence

4 Season Tailoring & Dry Cleaning has been a trusted name in the Orleans community since 2005. Located in the Avalon Centre on Tenth Line Road, we have spent over two decades perfecting our craft and building lasting relationships with our customers.

With an impressive 110 reviews and a 4.4-star rating, we are one of the most reviewed and trusted tailoring businesses in the Ottawa region. Our reputation is built on consistent quality, honest pricing, and a genuine commitment to making every customer look and feel their best.

Whether you need a simple hem, complex alterations on formal wear, or professional dry cleaning for your most delicate garments, our experienced team handles every piece with skill and care. We take pride in our fast turnaround times and our ability to tackle even the most challenging tailoring jobs.

What We Offer

Our Services

Tailoring

Custom fitting and garment construction for all occasions. From casual wear to formal suits, we craft the perfect fit every time.

Alterations

Expert alterations for pants, skirts, dresses, suits and more. We resize, reshape, and perfect your garments with precision.

Dry Cleaning

Professional dry cleaning services using gentle, fabric-safe methods. We protect your investment while restoring garments to pristine condition.

Zipper Repair

Broken zipper? We replace and repair zippers on jackets, pants, bags, and boots. Quick turnaround with quality results.

Bridal Alterations

Make your wedding day perfect with expertly fitted bridal gowns and bridesmaid dresses. Delicate fabrics handled with care.

Leather & Suede

Specialized cleaning and repair for leather and suede garments. We restore your premium pieces to their original beauty.
