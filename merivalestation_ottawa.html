A Family Kitchen, Open to Everyone

For over 25 years, Merivale Station Restaurant has been a beloved neighbourhood staple in Ottawa's Nepean area. Owned and operated by Isa and Merita, our kitchen runs on one simple principle: serve real, home-cooked food that fills your plate and warms your heart.

From our legendary fish and chips to our hearty all-day breakfasts, every dish is prepared fresh with the care and attention you'd expect from a family kitchen. Our regulars come for the food, but they stay for the feeling — a place where the owners know your name and your plate is always full.

Whether you're starting your day with a classic breakfast or grabbing a satisfying lunch, we're here to make sure you never leave hungry.
