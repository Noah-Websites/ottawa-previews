About Us

Your Neighbourhood Market in Barrhaven

Tia Mart has been proudly serving the Barrhaven community for years, providing families with fresh halal meats, quality produce, and a wide range of international grocery products. Located on Strandherd Drive in the heart of Barrhaven, we are the neighbourhood market that feels like home.

Our butcher counter is the centrepiece of our store, offering premium halal cuts of chicken, beef, lamb, and goat prepared fresh every day. Our experienced butchers are happy to prepare custom cuts, marinate meats, or recommend the best options for your next meal. We take pride in the quality and freshness of every product that leaves our counter.

Beyond the butcher counter, our shelves are stocked with specialty cheeses, dry goods, fresh vegetables, and hard-to-find international products. With over 267 Google reviews and a loyal following of 1,400 Facebook fans, Tia Mart has become the go-to destination for quality halal groceries in south Ottawa. We are grateful for our community and look forward to serving you.
