About Us

Craftsmanship Meets Care

Ottawa France Tailoring & Dry Cleaning has been a cornerstone of the Alta Vista Shopping Centre for over a decade. Our skilled artisans bring a European tradition of fine craftsmanship to every garment that comes through our doors.

With a stellar 4.8-star rating from 78 satisfied customers, we are known across Ottawa for our precision alterations, bespoke tailoring, and meticulous dry cleaning services. Whether you need a suit perfectly fitted, a wedding dress altered, or your finest garments carefully cleaned, we deliver results that exceed expectations.

Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has earned us one of the highest ratings among tailors in the Ottawa region. We treat every piece with the respect and attention it deserves, using time-tested techniques and modern care methods to protect your valuable garments.

What We Offer

Our Expert Services

Custom Tailoring

Bespoke garments crafted to your exact measurements. From suits and shirts to dresses, we bring your vision to life with precision.

Alterations

Expert alterations for all garment types. Hemming, tapering, letting out, taking in, and resizing done with meticulous attention to detail.

Dry Cleaning

Professional dry cleaning that preserves your garments. We use gentle, effective methods to keep your clothes looking their absolute best.

Suit Fitting

Achieve the perfect fit for your suits and formal wear. Our expert tailors ensure every seam, lapel, and cuff falls exactly right.

Wedding Attire

Bridal gown alterations and formal wear adjustments for your special day. We handle delicate fabrics with the care they deserve.

Garment Repair

Breathe new life into damaged garments. Zipper replacements, patching, re-lining, and invisible mending by experienced hands.
