About Us

A Taste of Ethiopia in Ottawa

Welcome to Habesha Restaurant, where we bring the vibrant flavors and rich traditions of Ethiopian cuisine to the heart of Vanier. Since opening our doors, we have been dedicated to serving our community with authentic dishes prepared using time-honored recipes passed down through generations. Our passion is to share the warmth and hospitality of Ethiopian culture with every guest who walks through our doors.

Our signature experience centers around injera, the spongy, fermented flatbread that serves as both plate and utensil in traditional Ethiopian dining. Each injera is crafted fresh daily using traditional methods, providing the perfect foundation for our carefully curated selection of stews, proteins, and vegetables. We source the finest ingredients to ensure that every bite transports you to the highlands of Ethiopia.

Beyond our delicious platters, Habesha Restaurant is proud to present our traditional Ethiopian coffee ceremony. This sacred ritual, steeped in centuries of tradition, is more than just serving coffee; it is a celebration of community, friendship, and connection. Join us to experience this unique cultural tradition alongside our exceptional cuisine and warm, welcoming hospitality.
