About Our Store

Your Neighbourhood Grocery Hub

For over 3 years, Korems Foods & Variety Store has been serving the Gloucester community with a carefully curated selection of African, Caribbean, and Canadian products. Located on Montreal Road in Cyrville, we've become a trusted destination for families seeking authentic ingredients and specialty foods.

From our famous warm Jamaican patties to premium African specialty foods and fresh tropical produce, we stock everything you need. Whether you're searching for hard-to-find spices, quality meats, fresh vegetables, or everyday essentials like batteries and household items, you'll find it here at competitive prices.

More than just a grocery store, Korems is a community gathering place where people connect over shared cultural heritage and quality products. Visit us today and experience the warmth of authentic neighbourhood service.
