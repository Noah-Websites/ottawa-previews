Our Story

Honest Craftsmanship in the Heart of the Market

Welcome to Bytown Shoe Repair, where Joseph brings decades of expertise to every pair of shoes that walks through our door. Located in the vibrant ByWard Market neighbourhood, we've become Ottawa's trusted destination for quality shoe repair and leather restoration.

At Bytown Shoe Repair, we believe in the value of quality craftsmanship. Rather than discarding worn shoes, we give them new life through expert repairs, resoling, and restoration. Each project is handled with meticulous attention to detail, using traditional cobbling techniques combined with modern materials and tools.

Whether your favourite leather boots need new soles, your dress shoes require professional restoration, or you need specialized leather work, Joseph's commitment to excellence ensures your footwear receives the care it deserves. We take pride in being part of Ottawa's community, serving locals and visitors alike who appreciate the value of quality repair work.

Our Services

Shoe Repair & Resoling

Expert sole and heel replacement for dress shoes, casual wear, and boots. We use premium materials to ensure durability and comfort for years to come.

Leather Restoration

Bring your leather shoes and accessories back to life. We clean, condition, and restore leather to its original beauty and suppleness.

Zipper Replacement

Professional zipper repairs and replacements for boots, bags, and jackets. We ensure smooth operation and lasting quality on every fix.

Boot Repair

Specialized repairs for work boots, winter boots, and hiking footwear. We handle everything from sole replacement to structural reinforcement.

Bag & Belt Repair

Expert restoration of leather bags, belts, and other accessories. We repair straps, buckles, and structural damage with precision and care.

Key Cutting

Quick and accurate key cutting service for house keys, office keys, and more. Professional equipment ensures precision on every cut.
