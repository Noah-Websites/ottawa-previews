About Bytown Shoe Repair

Established over 15 years ago, Bytown Shoe Repair has become a trusted landmark in Ottawa's vibrant ByWard Market. Under the expert care of Joseph, our master cobbler, we've built a reputation for honest assessments and fair pricing that keeps customers returning for their most valued footwear.

We specialize in comprehensive shoe repair, leather restoration, and custom modifications. Every pair that enters our shop receives Joseph's personal attention and expertise. From everyday wear to cherished heirloom shoes, we approach each project with the same dedication to quality craftsmanship.

Our loyal customer base spans throughout Lowertown and beyond, trusting us with boots, dress shoes, leather goods, and unique pieces that deserve expert care. We believe that quality repairs extend the life of your favorite shoes while honoring the craftsmanship of their original makers.

Our Services

Shoe Repair

Professional repairs for all shoe types. Heel replacement, sole repairs, stitching work, and structural fixes to restore your footwear.

Leather Restoration

Expert restoration of leather bags, jackets, and accessories. Cleaning, conditioning, color repair, and structural restoration.

Boot Resoling

Complete resoling services for boots and hiking shoes. Quality materials and lasting construction for durability and comfort.

Zipper Replacement

Replace broken zippers on boots, bags, and leather jackets with quality hardware that matches your original piece.

Leather Dyeing

Professional leather dyeing and color correction. Refresh faded leather or change the color to breathe new life into your items.

Custom Modifications

Bespoke modifications tailored to your needs. From comfort adjustments to custom strap additions and structural changes.
