About Us

Our Story

Serving Ottawa Since 1995

City View Barber Shop has been a cornerstone of the Ottawa community for nearly three decades. Located in the heart of the City View neighborhood, we pride ourselves on delivering authentic, old-school barbershop service combined with modern grooming techniques.

Our experienced barbers specialize in classic cuts, precise fades, and timeless styles. We believe in taking the time to listen to each client's needs and deliver results that exceed expectations.

Whether you're looking for a fresh haircut, a professional beard trim, or the luxury of a hot towel shave, City View Barber Shop is your destination for genuine craftsmanship and friendly service.
