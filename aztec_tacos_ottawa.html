Authentic Flavours, Humble Roots

At Aztec Tacos, we believe in the power of tradition. Our taqueria is a small, intimate space with just 30 seats, designed to feel like home. Every dish is made with care and authenticity, honoring time-tested recipes passed down through generations in Mexico.

We specialize in 12 varieties of fresh tacos, each priced at just $5 each, made with hand-pressed corn tortillas prepared daily. Our commitment to quality means every ingredient is selected for its flavor and freshness, ensuring that every bite tastes like it was made in someone's family kitchen.

Every Saturday, we feature a rotating specialty menu with hearty platters and birria by the litre, giving you the chance to explore authentic Mexican cuisine beyond our daily offerings. Whether you're looking for a quick lunch or a taste of home, Aztec Tacos is your destination for real Mexican street food.

What We Offer

Signature Tacos

Choose from 12 authentic varieties, all made fresh daily with hand-pressed corn tortillas and traditional Mexican recipes. Just $5 per taco.

Tamales & Tacos Dorados

Authentic tamales and crispy fried tacos prepared the traditional way, bringing you flavors straight from Mexico.

Saturday Specials

Every Saturday, enjoy our rotating specialty menu featuring hearty platters and unique dishes you won't find during the week.

Birria by the Litre

Rich, slow-cooked birria available by the litre for special occasions and gatherings. Perfect for sharing with family and friends.

Catering & Party Platters

Need to feed a crowd? We offer catering services and party platters for any occasion. Contact us for custom orders.

Dine-In & Takeout

Enjoy your meal in our cozy 30-seat dining space or take it to go. We offer both convenient options for your lifestyle.
