About Us

Your Trusted Tailor Since 2019

Top Stitch Tailoring has been serving the Carling and Westboro communities for over five years with exceptional attention to detail and craftsmanship. We pride ourselves on understanding that every garment tells a story, and we're committed to preserving and enhancing that story through our professional alterations and tailoring services.

Our specialization in leather and fur alterations sets us apart from conventional tailoring shops. Whether you need delicate leather jacket adjustments, fur coat repairs, or intricate detailing work, our experienced team brings expertise and precision to every project. We understand the unique requirements of working with luxury materials and treat each piece with the utmost care.

From wedding dress alterations to winter jacket modifications, men's suit tailoring to bridesmaid dress adjustments, we handle all types of garments with the same level of dedication and excellence. We believe that well-fitted clothing builds confidence, and our mission is to ensure every client leaves our shop feeling absolutely perfect in their altered garments.

Our Services

What We Offer

Leather Alterations

Specialized alterations for leather jackets, coats, and accessories. We handle repairs, resizing, and custom modifications with precision and care for premium leather materials.

Fur Garment Repair

Expert restoration and alteration of fur coats, stoles, and accessories. Our skilled craftspeople ensure your luxury furs are beautifully maintained and expertly adjusted.

Suit Tailoring

Professional alterations for men's and women's suits. From hemming and tapering to jacket adjustments, we ensure the perfect fit for every body type and style preference.

Wedding Dress Alterations

Specialized bridal services for wedding and bridesmaid dresses. We handle hem adjustments, bustle installation, and delicate beading with exceptional attention to detail.

Winter Jacket Alterations

Expert modifications for winter coats, parkas, and cold weather outerwear. Hemming, sleeve adjustments, and insulation preservation are handled with care and expertise.

General Alterations

Comprehensive tailoring services for all garment types. Hemming, tapering, zipper replacement, and custom adjustments to ensure perfect fit for everyday clothing.
