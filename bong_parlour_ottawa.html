Bong Parlour

Ottawa's Premier Destination for Quality Glassware & Accessories

Curated Quality, Expert Advice

Bong Parlour is the premier smoke shop in Ottawa's Vanier neighborhood, dedicated to providing the finest selection of premium glass art and smoking accessories. With over a decade of passion for quality and customer satisfaction, we've built our reputation on trust and excellence.

Our carefully curated inventory features a wide selection of high-quality glassware, vaporizers, rolling papers, and accessories from trusted brands. Whether you're a seasoned collector or just starting your journey, our knowledgeable staff is here to guide you through our extensive selection and help you find exactly what you need.

We're a locally owned and operated business committed to fostering a friendly, welcoming atmosphere where all customers feel valued. Visit us at 255 Montréal Rd Suite 207 to experience our exceptional service and discover why Bong Parlour is Ottawa's preferred destination for quality smoke shop products.
