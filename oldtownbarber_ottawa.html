About Old Town

Since establishing our chair in the heart of Nepean, Old Town Barber Shop has become the go-to destination for men seeking authentic grooming experiences. We're not just a barbershop—we're a community hub where style, tradition, and urban culture converge.

Our master barbers bring decades of combined experience, trained in both classic barbering techniques and contemporary styling. Every customer walks out the door looking sharp and feeling confident.
