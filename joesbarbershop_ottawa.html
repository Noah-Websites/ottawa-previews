Our Heritage

45+ Years of Tradition

Since 1979, Joe's Barber Shop has been the trusted destination for gentlemen's grooming in Eastern Ottawa. Located in the heart of Place d'Orléans Shopping Mall, we've built our reputation on precision, professionalism, and timeless style.

From classic scissor cuts to contemporary fades, our master barbers combine traditional techniques with modern trends. We believe in the art of barbering—each cut is a statement, each shave is an experience.

Whether you're a longtime customer or visiting for the first time, you'll discover why generations of Ottawans have made Joe's their barber shop of choice.
