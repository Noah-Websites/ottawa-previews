About Us

Kanata's Favourite Halal Market

Shirin Market has been serving the Kanata community for over a decade, providing families with the freshest halal meats and a carefully curated selection of Mediterranean, Middle Eastern, and South Asian groceries. Located on March Road, we have become the go-to destination for quality halal products in west Ottawa.

Our butcher counter features premium cuts of lamb, beef, chicken, and goat, all hand-selected and prepared to our exacting standards. Whether you need a rack of lamb for a weekend dinner or ground beef for a weeknight meal, our experienced butchers are here to help you choose the perfect cut and prepare it exactly the way you like.

Beyond our meat counter, our shelves are stocked with authentic products from Iran, Turkey, India, and across the Middle East. From fragrant saffron and premium basmati rice to specialty cheeses and freshly baked breads, Shirin Market brings the flavours of home to Kanata. We are proud to be part of this community and look forward to serving you.

What We Offer

Quality Products, Every Visit

Premium Halal Meats

Hand-selected cuts of lamb, beef, chicken, and goat. Our butchers prepare every order with care and precision to your specifications.

International Groceries

A wide selection of products from Iran, Turkey, India, and the Middle East. Find everything from spices and rice to specialty sauces and condiments.

Dairy and Cheese

Imported and local cheeses including feta, halloumi, labneh, and more. We carry a variety of Mediterranean and Middle Eastern dairy products.

Rice, Grains and Legumes

Premium basmati rice, lentils, chickpeas, and specialty grains sourced from trusted suppliers for authentic home cooking.

Fresh Produce

Seasonal fruits and vegetables alongside specialty items like fresh herbs, pomegranates, dates, and other hard-to-find produce.

Sweets and Snacks

Traditional Middle Eastern sweets, cookies, Turkish delight, and a variety of international snacks for every taste and occasion.
