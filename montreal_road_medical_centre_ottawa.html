Your Health, Our Priority

Montreal Road Medical Centre has been serving Ottawa families for over 15 years with compassionate, comprehensive healthcare. Our walk-in clinic welcomes all ages and provides quick access to quality medical care without the need for an appointment.

We specialize in family medicine, pediatric care, travel medicine, and minor surgical procedures. Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals is committed to providing thorough, personalized care to every patient in a welcoming environment.

Located on the vibrant Montreal Road in Vanier, we're conveniently accessible and currently accepting new patients. We offer mental health counselling services and are here to support your entire family's health and wellness journey.

Our Services

Family Medicine

Comprehensive primary care for patients of all ages, from preventive medicine to chronic disease management.

Walk-In Clinic

No appointment needed. We welcome walk-ins and offer quick access to healthcare when you need it most.

Pediatric Care

Specialized healthcare for children and adolescents, including vaccinations, growth monitoring, and developmental assessments.

Travel Medicine

Pre-travel consultations, vaccinations, and health advice to ensure safe and healthy journeys around the world.

Minor Surgeries

Professional in-office procedures for minor surgical needs with expert care and attention to detail.

Counselling Services

Mental health support and counselling to help you navigate life's challenges and improve your overall wellness.
