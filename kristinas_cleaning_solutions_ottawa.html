About Us

Trusted Cleaning Since 2020

Kristina's Cleaning Solutions was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to help Ottawa families reclaim their space and peace of mind. As a women-owned business, we take immense pride in delivering cleaning services that go well beyond surface-level tidying.

Over the past six years, we have earned a perfect 5-star rating by treating every home and office as if it were our own. Our team uses eco-friendly products, pays meticulous attention to detail, and always leaves your space feeling refreshed and inviting.

Whether you need a one-time deep clean, regular residential maintenance, or help decluttering and organizing your living space, Kristina's Cleaning Solutions is the trusted Ottawa choice. We serve the entire Ottawa and Gatineau area with flexible scheduling and honest, transparent pricing.
