A Taste of Seoul in the Heart of Ottawa

At K, Let's Eat, we believe Korean food is meant to be shared — with big portions, bold spices, and a whole lot of heart. Our kitchen brings together classic Korean recipes and Chinese-Canadian fried chicken traditions to create something truly special.

Every dish is prepared fresh, from our crispy double-fried chicken to our signature kimchi fried rice. Whether you're grabbing a quick lunch or settling in for a feast with friends, we're here to make sure you leave happy and full.

Located on Preston Street in Ottawa's vibrant Little Italy neighbourhood, we've become a favourite for locals craving authentic Korean comfort food at honest prices.
