Our Story

A Taste of Tradition on Bank Street

Sindbad Bakery brings the rich flavours of the Middle East to the heart of Ottawa's Centretown. Every morning, our bakers arrive before dawn to prepare a fresh selection of breads, pastries, and sweets using time-honoured recipes passed down through generations. The aroma of warm pita and savoury manakish fills Bank Street from the moment we open our doors.

We believe great baking starts with quality ingredients and patience. Our baklava is layered by hand with pure butter and premium pistachios. Our kunafa is made with the finest shredded phyllo and sweet cheese. Every pie, from zaatar to spiced lamb, is crafted with the same care you would find in a family kitchen in Beirut or Damascus.

Whether you are picking up fresh bread for dinner, choosing a tray of sweets for a celebration, or simply stopping in for a warm manakish on your lunch break, Sindbad Bakery is here to make your day a little more delicious. We are proud to serve Ottawa's diverse community and share the flavours we grew up with.

What We Offer

Fresh From Our Ovens

Fresh Breads

Warm pita, taboon, and flatbreads baked throughout the day. Perfect for meals, sandwiches, or dipping with hummus and labneh.

Savoury Pies

Handmade manakish topped with zaatar, cheese, or spiced meat. Our savoury pies are baked to order and ready in minutes.

Baklava and Sweets

Layers of delicate phyllo, pure butter, and premium nuts soaked in fragrant syrup. Available by the piece or in gift trays.

Kunafa and Basbousa

Traditional Middle Eastern desserts made fresh daily. Our kunafa features golden crispy vermicelli with sweet stretchy cheese inside.

Custom Catering Trays

Hosting a party or event? Order custom trays of assorted sweets, savoury pastries, or fresh breads for any occasion.

Delivery Available

Order your favourite items through SkipTheDishes or Uber Eats and have fresh baked goods delivered right to your door.
