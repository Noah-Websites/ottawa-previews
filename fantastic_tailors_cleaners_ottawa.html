About Us

Fantastic Tailors & Cleaners has been a trusted fixture in the Vanier neighborhood for over 10 years. Located on Montreal Road, we have built our reputation on providing exceptional tailoring and dry cleaning services to our community.

Our team of skilled professionals takes pride in every garment that comes through our doors. Whether you need precise alterations, professional pressing, or expert dry cleaning, we deliver results that exceed expectations.

We understand that quality clothing deserves quality care. That's why we use the finest techniques and premium materials to ensure your garments look and feel their best, no matter what service you require.

Our Services

Suit Alterations

Expert tailoring for perfect fit. We handle jacket adjustments, trouser hemming, and sleeve alterations with precision and care.

Dress Alterations

Transform your dresses to fit perfectly. From hems to take-ins, we ensure every alteration enhances your garment's appearance.

Dry Cleaning

Professional dry cleaning using advanced techniques to preserve fabric quality and extend the life of your favorite clothes.

Garment Pressing

Expert pressing services to remove wrinkles and restore the crisp appearance of your garments in time for special occasions.

Hem and Repair

Skilled repairs for torn seams, broken zippers, and general garment maintenance to keep your clothes looking fresh.

Seasonal Garment Care

Prepare your wardrobe for the season with our specialized cleaning and storage solutions for winter coats and summer wear.
