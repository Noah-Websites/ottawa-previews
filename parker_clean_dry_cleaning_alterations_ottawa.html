About Us

Quality You Can Trust

Parker Clean has been a reliable neighbourhood fixture on Charlemagne Boulevard in the Chapel Hill South area of Orleans for over a decade. We specialize in professional dry cleaning and skilled alterations, combining modern cleaning techniques with old-fashioned attention to detail.

Our 56 Google reviews and 4.1-star rating reflect our dedication to consistent quality and friendly service. Customers across Orleans trust us with their most important garments, from everyday work clothes to cherished formal wear and delicate fabrics.

Located next to Orleans Home Hardware, we offer easy access and ample parking. Whether you need a quick press, detailed alterations on a special outfit, or regular garment cleaning, Parker Clean delivers reliable results at fair prices every time.

What We Offer

Our Services

Dry Cleaning

Professional dry cleaning for all garment types. Suits, dresses, coats, and delicates handled with expert care and attention to fabric needs.

Alterations

Precision alterations to ensure the perfect fit. From simple hemming to complex restructuring, our skilled tailors deliver exceptional results.

Shirt Service

Professional laundering and pressing for dress shirts. Crisp collars, smooth fronts, and perfectly pressed sleeves every time.

Pressing & Steaming

Remove wrinkles and restore garments to a polished, professional look. Ideal for suits, formal wear, and interview outfits.

Zipper Repair

Fast, reliable zipper replacement on jackets, pants, bags, and more. We match zipper colour and style for a seamless repair.

Formal Wear Care

Specialized cleaning and preservation for wedding dresses, tuxedos, and formal attire. Gentle handling for your most precious garments.
